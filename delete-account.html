Delete Your Account & Data

Sakura Wellness Studio

This page explains how to request deletion of your Sakura Wellness Studio account and the personal data associated with it, in accordance with our commitment to your privacy.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to aeasyihub@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete My Account".
  3. Include your full name and the phone number registered with the studio so we can verify your identity.

We will process your request and permanently delete your account within 30 days of verification. You will receive a confirmation email once completed.

Contact for deletion requests
aeasyihub@gmail.com

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the following:

  • Your personal information (first name, last name, email address, phone number)
  • Your body measurement and fitness-tracking data
  • Your class bookings and attendance records
  • Your package and session usage records
  • Your login credentials and push notification tokens

What Data May Be Retained

For legal, accounting, and tax compliance, we may retain limited transaction and payment records for up to the period required by applicable law (typically up to 10 years). These records are kept solely to meet legal obligations and are not used for any other purpose. All other personal data is deleted as described above.
